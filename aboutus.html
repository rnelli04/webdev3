History
The Indian Premier League (IPL) was founded in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). It was created to promote fast-paced T20 cricket and has grown into one of the most popular sports leagues in the world.
Organization
The IPL is organized by the BCCI and governed by the IPL Governing Council. It follows a franchise-based model and features teams representing major Indian cities.
Current Teams (2025)
Chennai Super Kings, Mumbai Indians, Royal Challengers Bengaluru, Kolkata Knight Riders, Sunrisers Hyderabad, Gujarat Titans, Rajasthan Royals, Delhi Capitals, Punjab Kings, Lucknow Super Giants
